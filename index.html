About Me

With more than two decades of experience in the tech industry, I've learned that the core of my work is creating solutions that not only address business challenges but also inspire innovation. As a TOGAF-certified Enterprise Architect with a deep understanding of cloud platforms like AWS and Azure, I've had the privilege of working across diverse sectors, including the public sector, telecom, banking, and insurance.

At CAST, I lead presales engagements for public sector organizations, as well as large and medium-sized enterprises across India, the Middle East, and the APAC region. In this role, I collaborate closely with clients to understand their unique business needs and design tailored solutions that modernize their IT landscapes through our cutting edge Software Intelligent platforms. Whether it's helping organizations transition to the cloud, optimize their existing application code, or innovate with cutting-edge technologies, I ensure that every solution aligns with their strategic goals.

My previous roles as Solutions Architect at Happiest Minds Technologies and Technical Leader at Atos International further shaped my expertise in IT transformation and custom application development. These experiences have allowed me to fine-tune my skills in designing scalable, secure, and future-ready solutions.
